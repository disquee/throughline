# VenueOS Product Knowledge Base

This is the published layer of the VenueOS PKB: the curated and original content that GTM teams (Sales, Marketing, Customer Success, Professional Services) read directly.

It is one part of a larger system:

- **Signal capture** pulls raw context from Jira, Confluence, and meeting notes into a content store.
- **Routing** decides what gets auto-generated, curated, or authored.
- **This site** publishes the curated and original slice, versioned and reviewed like code.
- **An MCP server** lets Claude retrieve across the whole store (raw signal plus published pages) and answer GTM questions with citations.

Start with the [Smart Hold feature](/features/smart-hold), the headline of the [2026.2 release](/releases/2026-2).
